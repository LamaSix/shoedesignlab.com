The Shoe Design Lab is an immersive experience for young adults on the design & development of sneakers, created by LAMA SIX.

Please review the current workshop brief.

Call for donations

This workshop was created to teach design and development principals to young adults, and does not generate commercial revenue or monetize its offering in any way. As such, we are seeking partners to donate materials or cover the costs of materials to provide the best opportunities and experiences possible for the participants. Ideally a partner in the footwear industry could provide the best scrap or new materials for our workshops.

If you or your company would like to help us by donating materials or covering the costs of materials, please contact us at hi@lamasix.com

Workshop Event

The current workshop was part of Berekely Carroll School's 2018 STEAM Fair. This event was only open to Berkeley Carroll students, families and alumni.

We would love to organize future workshop events, please contact us if you are interested in hosting this workshop.
